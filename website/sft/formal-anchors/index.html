Audit scope

This is the Lean-facing audit surface for SFT. It does not replace the narrative Formal Core page or the forward-looking Theorem Candidates page. It records which current Lean declarations anchor the public SFT reading.

The pattern is the same as AAT: prose carries the theory's force, while Lean keeps the claim grammar bounded. A field is represented by selected state and projection data. A force is represented by support, policy, action, update, or observation boundary. A forecast is represented by finite path membership, not by point prediction.

Counterexamples

Claim group

Counterexample registry and non-conclusions

proved anchors

Audits Theorem Candidates counterexample anchors and the SFT status rule that forecasts, signatures, and reports do not silently promote into stronger claims.

Representative anchors

Current Lean status
