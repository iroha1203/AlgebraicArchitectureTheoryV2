Core artifacts

  • archmap-observation-map-v0Source-grounded atom, molecule, semantic observation, concern, gap, provenance, and non-conclusion records.
  • law-policy-v0Selected law universe, witness rules, molecule patterns, obstruction definitions, signature axes, coverage requirements, and exactness assumptions.
  • archsig-analysis-packet-v0Current source-of-truth output: molecule readings, obstruction circuits, signature axes, flatness reading, repair candidates, coverage gaps, LLM notes, and non-conclusions.
  • Analysis validationChecks references, law-relative obstruction links, signature and flatness refs, repair guardrails, coverage gaps, and non-conclusions.

Review artifacts

  • AIRBounded projection of analysis state into Architecture Interpretation Record form.
  • Theorem checkReports whether formal theorem-shaped readings have the needed assumptions and bridge evidence.
  • Feature reportTurns analysis-derived review state into reviewer-facing explanation of feature movement, witnesses, and inspection points.
  • AAT Observable BundleProjects analysis-derived concepts, witnesses, theorem boundaries, and responsibility boundaries into an AAT-facing review bundle.
  • Policy decision / PR commentHuman-facing review surfaces; neither is merge approval or theorem proof.

FieldSig artifacts

SFT forecast, governance, calibration, and operational artifacts are owned by FieldSig. The current handoff reads archsig-analysis-packet-v0 and emits operation-support-estimate-v0 with bounded obstruction, signature, repair, and coverage-gap refs.

  • Operation support estimateProjects ArchSig analysis state into selected operation-support input without forecast truth promotion.
  • Forecast cone skeletonRepresents reachable future regions for review and planning.
  • Consequence envelopeProjects likely review-relevant consequence regions and follow-up evidence needs.
  • AI proposal governanceRecords governance pressure around AI-generated proposals and required confirmation.

Retention

CI should retain enough artifacts to audit a review later: ArchMap, LawPolicy, validation reports, analysis packet, analysis validation, LLM interpretation packet, and any projected AIR, theorem-check, feature-report, observable-bundle, policy decision, or PR comment artifact.

Diagnostic reading

When an artifact looks wrong, first identify whether the problem is source observation, selected LawPolicy, analysis packet references, coverage gaps, schema compatibility, validation failure, or downstream projection. Do not debug a feature report before checking the analysis packet it came from.
